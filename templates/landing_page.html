Stock Streaming Web Application

Displays Stock Information about 25 Selected Stocks listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange

To view the stock information being streamed, click the "Launch table" button here >>>>

How it works:

This app displays the current closing stock information of 25 selected stocks. If the stock market is still opened as at the time the app is run, the previous day's closing information will be displayed.

The information displayed includes the following:

  1. The listed company's name
  2. Stock symbol
  3. Current closing price of the stock
  4. Change in stock price from the previous day (in figures and percentage)
  5. Last time within the day the stock price changed
Furthermore,the date at which the web page was last updated is displayed at the top.
